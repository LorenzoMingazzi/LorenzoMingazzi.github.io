Fracture Mechanics


This section focuses on the study of fracture mechanics, which investigates the behavior of materials and structures in the presence of cracks or other discontinuities. It explores how cracks initiate, propagate, and ultimately lead to failure under different loading conditions. Fracture mechanics is essential in understanding the durability and integrity of materials subjected to stress, and it plays a crucial role in fields such as aerospace, civil engineering, and materials science.
